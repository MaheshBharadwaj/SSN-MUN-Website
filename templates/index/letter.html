Letter from the Secretary General

November 05, 2021

Greetings Delegates,
I consider it an absolute honour to invite you to the 8th edition of SSN Model United Nations, set to take place on 8th & 9th January 2022, virtually and safely. Year after year, SSNMUN strives to be bigger and better, serving as a trendsetter for Model UN conferences across the South Indian circuit. I feel privileged to be returning to SSNMUN 2022 as Secretary-General. Our team at SSN College of Engineering has worked hard to overcome the barriers of the COVID-19 pandemic and organize a second virtual conference that is truly limitless in its potential.

This edition, we are simulating a combination of the most vital, yet unique and interesting committees. We have the UN Security Council (UNSC) which holds a special place in SSNMUN, with our stellar Executive Board who strive to keep it innovative and different year after year. We also have the UN Human Rights Council which serves as a great platform to encourage first-time participants and debate enthusiasts to discuss pressing human rights issues across the globe. The committees I am personally excited for this year are the San Francisco Conference of 1945 which will be a historic simulation giving every participating delegate to revisit the UN Charter itself; and the Observer Research Foundation (ORF), India’s largest think-tank, both committees recreated for the first time in SSNMUN history.

I urge all delegates to participate with an open mind, to imbibe a spirit of inquiry that starts with your research and extends to questioning, challenging and learning about global affairs and issues. We, at SSNMUN, believe that Model UNs are platforms that offer holistic development- be it enhancing your communication skills, augmenting your research capacities, overcoming your fears and throwing yourself at public speaking, lobbying and honing your diplomacy skills. I promise you that you will leave the conference with experiences of unparalleled debate quality, memorable deliberation and a heightened sense of diplomacy.

Looking forward to having you at the conference!


Warmest regards,
Kathiyayini Sivakumar
Secretary-General, SSNMUN 2022

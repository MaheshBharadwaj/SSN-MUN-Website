Pay to confirm your allotment

The payment portal for allotments is now open. All allotted delegates and reporters are expected to make their payment soon or risk losing their allotment. The delegate fees to be paid is 500 INR.

Instructions

  1. Click the payment link and you will be redirected to the PayTM portal for payment. Alternatively, you can also scan the QR code.
  2. Fill in your full name that you would like on your certificate, your email ID as well as your phone contact number.
  3. Under MUN Registration Number - Fill in the unique number that has been allocated to you.
  4. Under Name of Allotment - Fill in your committee followed by country ( e.g. SC USA, HRC Russia etc). Country is a MUST.
  5. The amount of Rs. 500 should already be entered in its field.
  6. Press proceed to complete your payment.
  7. Once the payment is complete, Please do send a screenshot of the payment receipt to ssnmun.contact@gmail.com. If you have trouble finding the payment receipt on PayTM, you can find it in the My Orders section under the registration fee transaction. Submission of the payment receipt is mandatory as it will enable us to confirm your payment with our received list and is the final step in sealing your allotment.
  8. In case of any queries, do feel free to contact prasidha1842@bme.ssn.edu.in .
